To generate the social preview image:

1. Open the social-preview.html file in your browser
2. Take a screenshot of the entire page (try to match the dimensions 1200x630px if possible)
3. Save the screenshot as "social-preview.jpg" in this folder

This image will be used when your website is shared on social media platforms.

Recommended dimensions: 1200x630 pixels (ratio 1.91:1)
